List of commands:
B#HELP~| - Will give you this list of instructions.
B#CLEAR~| - WIll clear the whole command line for you.
B#ROLL~| - Will roll the die and move you an according amount of units.
B#PLAYERS~| - Will show you the map and highlight where every player is.
B#LOCATIONS~| - Will show you the map and highlight where every ladder, snake, and redemption point is.
B#STATUS~| - Will show you how far you are from the end point.
B#SURRENDER~| - If you've lost all hope in victory, might as well admit it.
